Suya(Grilled Meat)

Description

Suya is traditional smoked spiced meat skewer which originates from Nigeria, and is a popular food item across West Africa. Suya is a large part of Hausa culture and food and is traditionally prepared and made by Hausa men, thus called 'Mai nama' or ‘Aboki’

Suya is generally made with skewered beef, ram, or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef Steaks thinly sliced
  • 2 Tablespoons of Souldeliciouz Suya Spice
  • 2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
  • Sliced Onions, Tomatoes and Cucumbers

List

  1. Preheat Oven to 190c or Gas Mark 5
  2. Place beef steaks in a bowl
  3. Rub the Suya Spice and Oil into the beef and leave for 1 hour or in a fridge overnight
  4. Place beef steaks in a greased foiled oven tray and cook for 20 – 25 mins checking halfway through to flip the beef over.
  5. Once beef has been cooked to your desired texture, remove from oven and sliced into strips.
  6. Serve your Suya with your onions, tomatoes and cucumber
